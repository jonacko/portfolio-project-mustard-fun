Bio:

    Robert James Smith (born 21 April 1959) is an English singer, songwriter, musician, and record producer. He is best known as the lead singer, guitarist, primary songwriter, and only continuous member of the rock band The Cure, founded in 1978. Smith is known for his guitar-playing style, distinctive voice, and fashion sense. He is a prominent figure within the Goth subculture.
